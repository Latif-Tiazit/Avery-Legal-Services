About Us

Welcome to Avery Legal Services

Avery Legal Services is a Paralegal litigation firm that embodies the best qualities of the Canadian spirit: tenacious, adaptable, ready for hard work, and committed to its causes. We assist individuals, small businesses and corporations to recover money owe to them and claim damages that may arise from their claims.

We currently provide legal services primarily in the Greater Toronto Area and its environs. When businesses and professionals need legal advice or representation, they turn to Avery Legal Services to provide the highest level of protection, with utmost discretion at a cost-effective rate.
